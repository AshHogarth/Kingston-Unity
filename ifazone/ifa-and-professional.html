IFA and Professional Introducer Document Library

All the documents you need are displayed here. If there is anything you require that isn't listed, please contact us.

IFA Documents:

Application Form - Investment NISA

Application Form to be used for NISAs. Direct Debit form available separately. To offer our products you will need to complete and return an agency agreement and signed Terms & Conditions.

Application Form - Investment Bond

Application Form to be used for Investment Bonds held in single or joint lives, with no trust documentation etc. Please use the complex form for bond applications with more than 2 lives assured/policyholders or where bond is to be held in trust. To offer our products you will need to complete and return an agency agreement and signed Terms & Conditions.

Application Form - Regular Savings Plan

Application Form to be used for Regular Savings Plan. Direct Debit form available seperately. The Medical Questionnaire will also need to be completed. To offer our products you will need to complete and return an agency agreement and signed Terms & Conditions.

Application Form - Tax Exempt Savings Plans

Application Form to be used for Tax Exempt Savings Plan (with or without life) and Child Tax Exempt Savings Plan. Direct Debit form available seperately. To offer our products you will need to complete and return an agency agreement and signed Terms & Conditions.

Bonus History and Useful Information

This document shows our latest bonus rates, bonus rate history and With Profits Fund asset allocation. For Financial Adviser use only.

Direct Debit Form

Direct Debit form for use with our regular premium plans. Please note we only take payments on 1st or 16th of each month. Please specify your client's preferred date on the application form.

Fee Facilitation Letter

A Word template of our fee facilitation letter which can be used on your own letterhead if required. This template contains all the information we require.

Fee Facilitation Notes

Financial Adviser notes for completion of our fee facilitation letter. A Client version is available on request.
