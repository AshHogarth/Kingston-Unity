Products

We offer RDR compliant products for advised business and Introducer products for clients you refer to us on an introduced basis. Application packs are available from the Document Library or via a link in each product section.

If you require specific illustrations or any further information please contact the New Business team via quotes@kingstonunity.co.uk or phone 01924 240164.

Please note that all application forms submitted to us for advised business must include evidence that verification of identity checks for anti-money laundering purposes have been carried out.

Investment Bond

A lump sum investment in our With Profits Fund with a guaranteed sum assured. There is also the option to take a regular income from the Bond.

Tax Exempt Savings Plan

Friendly Society Tax Exempt Savings Plan with or without life cover. Available to adults up to age 75 at entry or 65 if life cover chosen.

Investment NISA

A Stocks and Shares NISA that invests in the Kingston Unity With Profits Fund.

Child Tax Exempt Savings Plan

A Friendly Society Child Tax Exempt Savings Plan - offering the opportunity to build up a guaranteed lump sum for a child. Can be used to tie in with a major birthday eg aged 18, or can mature after a specific number of years (minimum 10).

Junior Investment NISA

For children who didn't qualify for a Child Trust Fund we have a Junior Investment NISA.

Regular Savings Plan

Ideal if clients have used their Friendly Society Tax Exempt Allowance - regular premiums from £15 - £250 per month. Life cover of up to £100,000 available.

Child Trust Fund

Although new Child Trust Fund Accounts ceased to be opened from Jan 2nd 2011, you can contact us for information about existing Child Trust Fund account holders including how to transfer a Child Trust Fund account to us from another provider and how to transfer an existing Child Trust Fund to a Junior ISA.
