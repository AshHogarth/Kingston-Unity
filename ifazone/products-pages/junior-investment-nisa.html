Products - Junior Investment NISA

If a child is aged under 16 and isn't eligible for a Child Trust Fund then a Junior NISA is an option. Children aged 16 and 17 can open a Junior NISA in their own name.

Our Junior Investment NISA is a Stocks & Shares NISA which invests in the Kingston Unity With Profits fund.

  • Available to children aged under 18 who don't qualify for a Child Trust Fund
  • Grows through the addition of potential bonuses
  • Any bonus declared is applied to the account annually
  • Bonus rates are not guaranteed, but any bonus that is added to the account is guaranteed to be paid to the child, along with return of capital if the Junior Investment NISA remains in the With Profits fund and is redeemed on the child's 18th birthday

Bonus rates are dependent on the performance of the With Profits fund and may be higher or lower than those declared in previous years. It is important to remember that past performance is not a guarantee of future performance.

A market value reduction may be applied if the child's Junior NISA is transferred to another provider before the child reaches the age of 18.
