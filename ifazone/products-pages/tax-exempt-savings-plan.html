Products - Tax Exempt Savings Plan

  • Tax Exempt (within the fund and on maturity)
  • Available to all UK clients from 0 to 75 at entry or 65 if life cover chosen. For children we offer the Child Tax Exempt Savings Plan.
  • Guaranteed sum assured at maturity
  • Addition of regular annual bonuses (not guaranteed)
  • Maturity proceeds of sum assured plus accrued bonuses
  • If death occurs during life of plan there will be return of premiums - or payment of sum assured plus bonuses for a plan with life cover.
  • Premiums from £15 to £25 per month
  • Minimum 10 year term
  • Term and premium fixed at onset
  • Invests in our With Profits Fund
  • Money Management With Profits Survey April 2015 confirms the Society's position as one of the leading performers for maturity pay-outs over 10, 15, 20 and 25 years.
